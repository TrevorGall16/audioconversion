Privacy & File Handling Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This document outlines how we handle the audio files you upload and the non-personal data collected during your session.

1. File Uploads and Security

All file uploads and downloads are secured via HTTPS encryption. Your audio files are processed in-memory or on temporary storage during conversion.

2. File Deletion Policy

We implement a strict no-retention policy:

  • Your uploaded audio file is **automatically and immediately deleted** from our servers after the conversion process is complete.
  • The converted file remains accessible for **a maximum of 1 hour** to allow for download, after which it is automatically and permanently deleted.
  • We do not store backups or copies of your files.

3. Data Collection

We do not require user registration or personal identification. We do not collect names, email addresses, or IP addresses that can be used to identify you.

4. AdSense and Cookies

This service is supported by Google AdSense. AdSense uses cookies for personalized advertising, but only after you provide explicit consent via the on-site banner. These cookies are subject to Google’s own privacy policies.
