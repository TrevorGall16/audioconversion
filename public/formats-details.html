Supported Audio Formats

MP3 (MPEG-1 Audio Layer III)

The most popular audio format. Best for sharing music and podcasts. Good balance of quality and file size.

WAV (Waveform Audio)

Uncompressed, lossless audio. Large file sizes but perfect quality. Used for professional editing.

FLAC (Free Lossless Audio Codec)

Compressed but lossless. You get perfect quality with a smaller file size than WAV.
